What is a Discovery Session?

A FREE day of consultation with our Seka Squared engineers and business experts.

Our specialists will invest one full day for you to understand the data and machine learning capabilities that can add value to your business right now, all at no charge. We also tailor our consultation session to accommodate the needs of our clients while covering the following topics:

Machine Learning & Data Science Overview

Seka Squared will present the current state-of-the-art in machine learning, big data and cloud computing and how data science is approached in South Africa.

Client needs identification

We consult with you to identify the role players, business processes, data architecture and data pipelines in your organisation.

Opportunity analysis

We help you to identify problems and opportunities that exist in your organisation were Data analysis and Machine Learning can assist.

Solution ideation

Together, we brain storm to find a machine learning solution to exploit opportunities and improve your organization.

Solution ranking

We help you evaluate different potential solutions and which technology stack may be most beneficial.

Automation journey

We conclude the day by identifying the next steps in your Machine Learning journey to implement solutions in your organisation.
